What Is Bioinformatics

February 12, 2024

Bioinformatics combines biology and computing to analyze biological data such as sequences, expression data, and protein structures.

Why it matters

  • Accelerates drug discovery and biomarker research.
  • Supports personalized medicine with genomic analysis.
  • Enables large-scale analysis impossible to do manually.

Core skills

  • Statistics and machine learning.
  • Programming in Python or R.
  • Strong foundation in molecular biology.
